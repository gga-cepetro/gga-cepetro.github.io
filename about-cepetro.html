About CEPETRO

The main purpose of the CENTER FOR PETROLEUM oSTUDIES is to provide a link between UNICAMP and society, in the petroleum area, by offering support to courses, technological and scientific research projects and services. CEPETRO is subordinated to the GENERAL COORDINATION OFFICE of UNICAMP, and supervised by a scientific council constituted of directors of institutes and faculties and external members.


History

CEPETRO was founded in 1987 and, in the same year, with the support of Petrobras, the Department of petroleum Engineering and the Master of Science course in Petroleum Engineering were created, both at the school of Mechanical Engineering. The Master of Science course in Geoengineering was created im 1990 at the Institute of Geosciences. The Ph.D. course in Petroleum Engineering started in 1993.

Nowadays, CEPETRO supports courses and projects in Petroleum Sciences and Engineering, mainly in Petroleum Exploitation and Reservoir Geoengineering, including Geology, Reservoir Engineering, Drilling and Well Completion, Petroleum Production and Petroleum Resources Management.

Among the course offered today by UNICAMP are the Master and PhD in Petroleum Sciences and Engineering Petroleum disciplines for undergraduates in Mechanical Engineering and the continuing education programs for governmental and industry professionals.
